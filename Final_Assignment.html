My Childhood

I was born in Dharwad, Karnatka India. It is a university town and known as educational capital. My father was a Civil Engineer with State Government, so we got to see a new place every three years courtesy Tax Payers' money. So I have lived and gown up in Gadag, Haveri, Belgaum, Mysore, Bangalore. My first job was in Pune Maharastra India. After couple of years I moved to Bangalore so that I could be with my parents and started working there. Then I moved to Mysore as I was tasked with leading a team based out of Mysore. From Mysore I came to US and have been in the US since then.
